Event Format

🗺️

Norman Split

Each runner starts with a pre-set Box and resources, then fights through the Norman Split: a specially-curated section of Pokémon Null ending with Gym Leader Norman as the final boss.

💀

Fewest Deaths Wins

The winner is whoever defeats Norman with the fewest Pokémon deaths. This is a strategy event, not a speedrun. We're here for the lines, not the clock. Time is only used as a tiebreaker.

📦

Equal Resources

All five runners receive the same Box of Pokémon, the same items, and the same preparation time. They'll get to choose their own Starter, but the only variables are skill and decision-making.
